

### always remeber the first immpretion is last immpration. 

## There are two types of communication
- verbal
- non-verbal (physical)

## verbal
- observe interviewers
- listen carefully
- allow the interviewer to direct the question to you completely before answering.
- use clear and consize language in answers.    
- dont exaggerate your abbulities.
- dont be negative about yourself.

## star method for answering the questions.
- Situation
    - what is the project? and what chalanges you faced?
- Task
    - what is your responsibilities? and assignments
- Action
    - what actions you take to rectify or  adress the challanges
- Result
    - what is the result or outcome of your actions.


## Non-verbal
- punctuality
- good practice to arrive 10mins before the interview.
- apprear composed and ready for interview not frustrated.
- maintain eye contact
- dress appropraite for interview.
- maintain good posture
- dont nurveusly touch your face during interview.
